About the Firm

A Client-Centered Firm Built on Lasting Relationships

Since its founding, Shehadeh Law Office has earned a reputation for client-centered, quality-conscious service. We believe the character of our client relationships makes us unlike most law firms.

A fundamental belief underpinning our work is that clients wish to minimize their involvement with the legal system. We work in partnership with our clients to avoid legal obstacles, and to handle legal problems in an efficient, professional manner when they do arise.
